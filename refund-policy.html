Refund Policy

1. General Refund Policy

At Knockly, we stand behind the quality of our work. However, due to the custom nature of web development services, our refund policy has specific conditions and timeframes that protect both our clients and our business.

2. Eligible Refund Situations

You may be eligible for a refund in the following circumstances:

  • Service Cancellation: Within 7 days of initial payment, before development work begins
  • Non-Delivery: If we fail to deliver agreed-upon services within reasonable timeframes
  • Technical Failure: If we cannot resolve critical technical issues that prevent your website from functioning
  • Material Breach: If Knockly fails to meet the fundamental terms of the service agreement

3. Non-Refundable Services

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Completed development work that meets agreed specifications
  • Third-party services, licenses, or subscriptions (hosting, domains, etc.)
  • Changes in client requirements after work has commenced
  • Client dissatisfaction with approved designs or functionality
  • Services used beyond the initial 7-day period
  • Custom work that has been deployed or delivered

4. Refund Timeframes

Refund eligibility is subject to specific timeframes:

  • 7-Day Window: Full refund available within 7 days of payment, before work begins
  • 14-Day Window: Partial refund may be available within 14 days for unresolved technical issues
  • Beyond 14 Days: Refunds only considered in cases of material breach or non-delivery

5. Partial Refunds

In some cases, partial refunds may be offered:

  • Calculated based on completed work and resources allocated
  • Deductions for third-party costs and non-recoverable expenses
  • Subject to assessment of work completed and time invested
  • Requires mutual agreement between client and Knockly

6. Refund Process

To request a refund:

  • Contact us at support@knockly.co.uk with your request
  • Provide detailed reasons for the refund request
  • Allow 5-10 business days for review and response
  • Approved refunds will be processed within 14 business days
  • Refunds will be credited to the original payment method

7. Service Alternatives

Before pursuing a refund, we encourage clients to consider:

  • Revision requests to address specific concerns
  • Alternative solutions that may meet your needs
  • Extended timelines for complex technical challenges
  • Consultation to clarify expectations and deliverables

8. Dispute Resolution

If you're unsatisfied with our refund decision:

  • Request a review with detailed documentation
  • Participate in good-faith mediation if necessary
  • Pursue resolution through appropriate legal channels
  • Contact relevant consumer protection agencies if applicable

9. Project Modifications

Changes to project scope may affect refund eligibility:

  • Scope increases may require additional payment and affect refund calculations
  • Scope reductions may result in service credits or partial refunds
  • Timeline changes due to client requests may void certain refund rights
  • All modifications should be documented in writing

10. Annual vs Monthly Billing

Refund terms differ based on billing frequency:

  • Annual Plans: Prorated refunds may apply for unused months after the first month
  • Monthly Plans: No refunds for partial months; service continues until end of billing period
  • Upgrades: Difference in cost is non-refundable once services are upgraded
  • Downgrades: Credit applied to future billing cycles

11. Force Majeure

Refunds may not be available if service delays or cancellations result from:

  • Natural disasters or extreme weather events
  • Government regulations or legal restrictions
  • Internet infrastructure failures or cyber attacks
  • Other circumstances beyond Knockly's reasonable control

12. Client Responsibilities

To maintain refund eligibility, clients must:

  • Provide timely feedback when requested
  • Respond to communications within reasonable timeframes
  • Supply necessary content and materials as agreed
  • Communicate concerns or issues promptly
  • Follow proper channels for refund requests

13. Refund Limitations

Please note the following limitations:

  • Maximum refund amount cannot exceed payments made to Knockly
  • Processing fees and transaction costs may be deducted
  • Currency conversion fees are non-refundable
  • Refunds do not include compensation for opportunity costs or damages

14. Contact for Refund Requests

For all refund enquiries and requests, please contact us at:

Email: support@knockly.co.uk

Please include your order information, detailed reason for the refund request, and any relevant documentation to expedite the review process.
